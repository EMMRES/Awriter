Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

Thank you for using AWriter. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AWriter collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address to register and manage your account.
  • Credentials: Your password is securely encrypted and cannot be accessed by us.
  • No Usage Tracking: AWriter does not track your activity, monitor your growth, or collect data on how you use the app. All features are fully private.
  • Advertising Data: AWriter displays ads through Google AdMob. We do not provide AdMob or any other service with personal information. AdMob may use general, non-personal device information to show ads, but AWriter itself does not collect, store, or sell any user data for advertising purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate and maintain your account.
  • Provide secure and reliable access to app features.
  • Process subscriptions and payments.
  • Ensure your experience in the app remains private; we do not track or share your information with advertisers.

3. Payments and Subscriptions

Payments are securely processed through Google Play or Apple App Store. AWriter never collects or stores any payment card information.

4. User Content

Your stories and outlines are private to your device. We never store or share your creative content.

5. Third-Party Services

We only use Google AdMob for ads and basic analytics. We never sell or share personal information.

6. Data Security

We use encryption and secure protocols to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us at info@awriterapp.com.
