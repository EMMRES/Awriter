Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Thank you for using AWriter. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address to register and manage your account.
  • Credentials: Your password is securely encrypted and cannot be accessed by us.
  • No Usage Tracking: AWriter does not track your activity, monitor your growth, or collect data on how you use the app.
  • Advertising Data: AWriter displays ads through Google AdMob. We do not provide AdMob with any personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain your account.
  • To provide secure and reliable access to app features.
  • To process subscriptions and payments.

3. Payments and Subscriptions

Payments are securely processed through Google Play or Apple App Store. AWriter never collects or stores any payment card information.

4. Data Security

We use encryption and secure protocols to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us at info@awriterapp.com.
